Game Opinions

What I Look for in a Video Game

For me, I like a game that has near endless replay value or customization. The more opportunities for freedom in how I choose to play, the more likely I am to enjoy the game, and keep coming back to it. Additionally, having multiplayer and being fun to play with my friends is another huge bonus.
